Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: your name, email address, and password when you register. We also store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. This includes authenticating your account, displaying your expense history, and generating spending summaries. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Expense data is associated with your account and is not accessible to other users.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect the security of the service.

5. Cookies and Sessions

Spendly uses a session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie contains no personal information and expires when you log out or close your browser. We do not use tracking or analytics cookies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Deleted data is removed from our systems within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also request a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, contact us through the details on our website.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, or loss. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, reach us through the contact details on our website.
